Global Business/Execution Models
Fixed Time, Fixed Price Model:

A low risk option, which you can choose when the scope and specifications for the projects are reasonably clear.

Dowell's process-optimization ensures reliability, predictability and optimized performance of the projects to deliver on time, on budget, on target.
Combine this with Dowell's Value for Money Offshore Development Center Execution Model and you have a very smart, unbeatable combination.

In this model, DOWELL will form the project team including the project management staff, equipment and infrastructure based on your projected requirements. You could pay the monthly charges based on the size and composition of the team. The model offers you flexibility to balance the size of your team with your projected workloads.

Most of the on-site services (unless it is part of a fixed price project) are delivered using this business model. DOWELL could bid for partial phases of a project on a Fixed Price basis and the remaining parts on a Time and Material basis if required.
