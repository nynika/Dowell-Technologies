About Us

DOWELL Technologies was established in 1984 at Chennai, India as Dowell Consultants by a group of Dynamic Software Professionals with rich experience in IT, driven by high values and integrity among themselves. We have positioned our self as an innovative leader in Software Services by consistently adapting to changes in business with all latest technologies. This strategy has enabled DOWELL to successfully execute any IT projects for the clients and bring in lot of values to their business in the past three & half decades.

We realize the importance of creating awareness among clients, about the improved power of Software in their new Business Process & Applications recommend an implementation plan to achieve a sustainable industry standard in all aspects to meet the growing challenges in the fast growing e-world. DOWELL has a long term objective of playing a Responsible Role in the IT Industry by offering reliable, consistent and quality services to clients at a reasonable cost.
