We provide you the best Training and Job experience

Dowell with its rich experience in IT Consulting, Education & Career building, prepare dynamic IT professionals to support the "Industry for The Next Generation".
The same is done as a free service to deserving young & aspiring professionals.


Objective : TO ENSURE THAT EVERY ELIGIBLE CANDIDATE THROUGH PROPER PROFESSIONAL TRAINING BE GIVEN THE CAREER OPPORTUNITY TO BE ASSOCIATED WITH REPUTED IT COMPANIES IN THE SHORTEST POSSIBLE TIME.





Our HRD Process:

* Candidates with an average of 80 % and above in X, XII and BE or MCA may please forward their resume to dowellhr@dowelltech.com.

*The eligible candidates will undergo rigorous Selection Process of written test, group discussion and technical interview.


*The selected candidates will then undergo On Job Training on technical skills.


*During the initial training period of three months they will be continuously assessed through internal assignments


*The candidates on completion of the initial three months training will undergo Live Project Training for a period of another three months.A nominal stipend may be paid to the deserving candidates during this 3 months period.


*These well trained IT professionals will then be placed in Dowell through an MOU.
